About

VitiCulture is the cultivation and harvesting of grapes for the production of wine by using grafting method. In viticulture computer vision, image processing and machine learning techniques are used for harvest yield estimation, vineyard management and monitoring, grape disease detection, quality evaluation and grape phenology. one more component Grape CS-ML database is used to store images of grape varities at different stages of development together with the corresponding ground truth data obtained from chemical analysis. This database is used to develop more practical solutions to deploy in smart vineyards.
